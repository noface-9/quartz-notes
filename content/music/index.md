## What is this?

For me, music is an integral part of life. Regardless of who we are, it has the remarkable ability to reach the deepest parts of us in ways that words often cannot. There is something profoundly intriguing about how sound, through nothing more than vibrations and frequencies, can evoke memories, stir emotions, and resonate so deeply within us.

I believe music has the power to soften even the hardest of hearts and connect us to something profoundly human.


## What you'll find here

I've had the longest on-and-off relationship with Carnatic music. Somehow, every time I return to it, I find myself starting from almost the same place—a beginner once again. As amusing as that is, I'm hopeful that documenting my journey here will help me stay with it a little longer each time, and perhaps eventually for good.

I have a deep admiration for devotional music. There is something profoundly moving about songs written out of love, surrender, and longing. I like to imagine that, one day, after enough practice, I might be able to sing well enough to bend down God's ear for a moment and make Him listen.

Beyond Carnatic music, I enjoy just about everything—pop, indie, country, EDM, blues, and anything soulful. This section is also my little catalogue of songs I'd love to learn and keep revisiting.

*(Though, for everyone's sake, perhaps it's best that they're heard by me before they're heard by anyone else. 😄)*
