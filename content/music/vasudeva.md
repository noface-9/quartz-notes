title: "Vasudeva"
---
```
1: Vasudeva kamalasana vandhita shesha shayana raaraa Krishna (2)
Dasuni nannitu mosamu chesithe doshamu neekeraa Gopala
Doshamu neekeraa Gopala… doshamu neekeraa

2: Mandaradhara Muchukunda varada sankrandana mrudu charanaa Krishna (2)
Brundaraka muni brunda vandita sundara ripu haranaa Gopala
Sundara ripu haranaa

3: Gatti vaadavanI pattithi nee cheyyi (2)
Patti viduvaraadu Krishna
Gatti vaadavanI pattithi nee cheyyi… patti viduvaraadu Krishna
Vatti mayalalo nannu tagilinchithe etula ne podu Gopala
Etula ne podu

4: Sharanu sharanu Bhooramana dayakaraa karuna jooda raaraa Krishna (2)
Dharanilona jana ooramuna velasina Narahari daya raadaa Gopala
Narahari daya raadaa Gopala… Narahari daya raadaa
```
