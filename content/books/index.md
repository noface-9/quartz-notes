## What is this?

I have a habit of reading books but haven't ever consolidated the takeaways at one place. Never have I ever written a solid review. Only time when my opinions are expressed is when I'm recommending books orally to my friends. This repo of mine is a great way to consolidate my views for my future self. 

## What you'll find here

Long rants and a quick peek into my head. 

These are books that have shaped my thinking. Rather than writing traditional reviews, I use these notes to capture themes, ideas, questions, and personal reflections.
